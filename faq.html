Welcome to 1pulse Online!

The heart beat of the community.

1pulse Online is a platform that shows an overview of how much work has been done in the community and how much work is yet to be done. The data is entered by community members and is used by community builders and businesses.

You can click on the specific spot on the map where you want to post your alert, fill in the details, and hit "Post". Your alert will show up for the community to see.

We keep posts until something is done about them or, in the case of events, until the event has ended.

The 1Pulse Community Help Desk serves as a vital resource for individuals and families in need, offering assistance in various areas to improve the quality of life within the community.

Its mission is to foster inclusivity, create safer environments, and enhance the overall well-being of the community, making it a central hub for those looking to make a positive impact.

The 1Pulse Community Development Hub is a cornerstone of empowerment and growth within the community, dedicated to providing essential resources and support for local development.

The hub focuses on building and improving infrastructure by sourcing and providing vital materials such as building supplies, equipment, and furniture. Through collective efforts, it aims to create spaces that foster collaboration, learning, and development for all members. .
